# Audit Scope and Context (Sample)

## Organization Description (Hypothetical)

A small technology company providing web-based services
with approximately 20 employees.

---

## Audit Scope

The scope of this internal audit covers:
- Information systems supporting web applications
- Employee access to internal systems
- Data stored on company servers

---

## Out-of-Scope Items

- Physical data centers managed by third parties
- Customer-managed devices

---

## Internal and External Issues

**Internal Issues:**
- Limited cybersecurity staff
- Increasing remote work

**External Issues:**
- Data protection regulations
- Rising cyber threats

---

## Interested Parties

- Customers
- Employees
- Management
- Regulatory bodies
