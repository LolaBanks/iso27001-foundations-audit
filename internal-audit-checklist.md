# Internal Audit Checklist (Sample)

This document is a **sample internal audit checklist**
created for learning and portfolio demonstration purposes.

It is designed to show how ISO/IEC 27001 requirements
can be verified during an internal audit.

⚠️ This is not a certified audit checklist.

---

## 1. Purpose of an Internal Audit

The purpose of an internal audit is to:

- Verify compliance with ISO/IEC 27001 requirements
- Confirm that security controls are implemented
- Identify gaps and improvement areas
- Ensure the ISMS is functioning as intended

Internal audits are required under **Clause 9.2 of ISO/IEC 27001**.

## 6. Auditor Comments (Sample)

The organization demonstrates basic alignment with ISO/IEC 27001
requirements. Opportunities exist to improve documentation consistency
and staff awareness.

---

## 7. Key Takeaways

- Internal audits are mandatory in ISO 27001
- Auditors verify evidence, not assumptions
- Documentation is critical
- Findings support continual improvement
